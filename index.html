QR Generator

About QR Generator

A simple Progressive Web App for generating QR codes from URLs. Perfect for sharing links quickly and easily.

How to Use

  1. Enter a valid URL in the input field
  2. Tap "Generate QR Code"
  3. Your QR code will appear below
  4. Tap "Download Image" to save the QR code

Features

  • Works offline after first load
  • Optimized for iOS and iPad
  • High-quality QR code generation
  • PNG image downloads

Privacy

All QR codes are generated locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.
